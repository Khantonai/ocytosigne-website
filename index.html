About Ocytosigne

Healthcare communication should be accessible to everyone. Ocytosigne was born from the understanding that hearing-impaired patients often face significant barriers when seeking medical care.

Our platform bridges this gap by providing healthcare professionals and patients with innovative tools for clear, effective communication. Through real-time sign language interpretation, visual aids, and accessible interface design, we ensure that every patient receives the care they deserve.
